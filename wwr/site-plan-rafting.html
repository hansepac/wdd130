Overview

Purpose

Entice/advertise a multi-location VR Rafting experience. Showcase the location/features, and sell tickets for the in person experience.

Audience

Gamers, People who enjoy White water Rafting, and locals. Gamers want immersive gaming experiences. People who enjoy white water rafting may be intrigued to see how it compares to the real deal, or to learn how to white water raft without experiencing the danger of it. Locals are intrigued by a new business.
